scipy.signal.residuez¶

scipy.signal.residuez(b, a, tol=0.001, rtype='avg')¶

Compute partial-fraction expansion of b(z) / a(z).

If M = len(b) and N = len(a)

b(z) b[0] + b[1] z**(-1) + ... + b[M-1] z**(-M+1)
